## Full Description
*(Max 4000 characters — shown on the app's store page)*

```
TaskRelay helps you stay on top of every task — even the ones you didn't finish yesterday.

Unlike basic to-do apps, TaskRelay gives each task a weight so you can manage your energy and time, not just your list. Set a daily capacity limit and let TaskRelay tell you when you've taken on too much. Tasks you don't finish automatically carry over to the next day, so nothing ever falls through the cracks — whether you missed one day or several.

───────────────────────────────────────
 SMART TASK MANAGEMENT
───────────────────────────────────────
• Assign a weight to every task and set your daily limit to avoid overloading yourself.
• Tasks left incomplete automatically roll over to the next day.
• Missed multiple days? TaskRelay traces the carry-over chain day by day so every unfinished task lands exactly where it should.
• Recurring tasks repeat daily without any extra setup.
• Organize tasks by type and priority.

───────────────────────────────────────
 ALARMS & REMINDERS
───────────────────────────────────────
• Set precise alarms for individual tasks — they fire even when your screen is off or your phone is locked.
• Full-screen alarm view wakes the screen and shows the task so you can act immediately.
• Mark tasks complete directly from the alarm without unlocking your phone.
• Schedule a daily reminder at a time that works for you.
• All notifications work completely offline — no account or internet required.

───────────────────────────────────────
 CALENDAR & INSIGHTS
───────────────────────────────────────
• Monthly calendar with color-coded days: green for completed, yellow for partial, red for missed.
• Tap any day to view or edit its tasks.
• Dashboard shows your streak, daily progress bar, and weekly completion stats at a glance.
• Smart suggestions warn you before you overcommit — like when carried-over tasks already exceed your daily limit.

───────────────────────────────────────
 PRIVATE & OFFLINE
───────────────────────────────────────
• All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.
• Works fully offline — no Wi-Fi, no account, no subscription.
• Lightweight and fast even with hundreds of tasks.

───────────────────────────────────────
 DESIGNED FOR DAILY USE
───────────────────────────────────────
• Clean Material Design 3 interface.
• Full dark mode support.
• Tasks survive device restarts — alarms are automatically rescheduled after reboot.

Download TaskRelay and start each day knowing exactly what needs to get done.
```
